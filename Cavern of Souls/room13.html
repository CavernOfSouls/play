You take a moment to rest - running for your life across the dripstone cave got you tired...

As you catch your breath, you take the time to investigate this new room you have found yourself into.

You see in the center a pedestal. Beyond it, the room splits into two forked corridors. At the end of each is a closed door - one is green, the other is red.

You take a closer look at the pedestal. On it, you see an inscription just above two dials. The inscription reads:

"We who have built the Cavern of Souls believe in Ashkanat, the one true God. No other deity stands to her might. Ye who wish to continue must be as devout as we are. If you know what Ashkanat demands as offering, the believer's door shall open. Else, you will perish by heretic's door! Praise be to Ashkanat!"

You take a closer look at the dials. Both are surrounded by a series of words. One points to foods, the other points to beverages. You understand that you need to select the two correct options to indicate what offering is made to Ashkanat.
