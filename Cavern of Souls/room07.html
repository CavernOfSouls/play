The Abyssals

An orange portal opened on the blank wall. As you crossed it, you felt it drain some of your energy.

You find yourself in a dark place. You cannot see much, but understand you are no longer in the Cavern of Souls.

As the portal closes behind you, you hear things moving around you in the distance.

In the total darkness, bright red flashes begin to appear. They do not last long, but for a brief moment they let you know what you are dealing with.

You recognize the creatures from legends:
they are Abyssals! Otherwordly horrors, best described as the things nightmares are affraid of.

Inbetween flashes, you can hear them creeping closer to you. You are surrounded. The red light they emit from their lures get brighter and brighter as they approach.

Suddently, a blinding light appears. As you realize one of the Abyssals is less than a meter in front of you, the sharp pain of its bite tearing your body appart is the last thing you will ever feel...

You Died!
