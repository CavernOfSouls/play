"Ye who is about to enter the lair of Belzarguur, demon guardian of the Sun Crystal of Ashkanat, beware.
Best be prepared, else best be praying! We grant you this armor in the hopes it will help smite the impure!".

You know now what lies ahead. One final task before you can claim your prize. One final decision before facing Belzarguur.
