The Banshee

You approach the woman and ask "Is there something wrong?"

She starts to cry louder. You ask again "Are you hurt? Do you need any assistance?"

She keeps ignoring you. Finally, you tap on her shoulder...

She abruptly turns around, and you scream in horror when seeing her face - A BANSHEE!

Her cries turn into a shriek! The sounds ruptures your eardrums...

Within seconds, your are paralyzed. At this moment, the Banshee jumps on you and starts eating you alive...

You Died!
