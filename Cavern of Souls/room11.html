The Hot Room

The deeper you go into the cavern's guts, the hotter it gets. Soon, you are having trouble breathing.

The stone walls around you are slowly turning incandescent red...

After several minutes going down, you still cannot see the end of the staircase. Realizing that going this way was a mistake, you decide to go back up.

Unfortunately, the extreme temperature is getting to you. You feel weak, about to faint. You try to drink water from your gourd, but it feels as if you just boiled it.

You realize that you will never be able to make it back up. So sad! Your journey had just begun...

You Died!
