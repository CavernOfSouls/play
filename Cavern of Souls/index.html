Cavern Of Souls

A Text-Based Dungeon Crawling Game

Welcome adventurer!

You have arrived to the Cavern of Souls. Somewhere inside lies a treasure beyond anything you can imagine:
a mystical crystal that shines as bright as the sun itself!

To get the crystal, you must first attempt to navigate across the cavern's maze-like paths, avoiding obstacles and maintaining your stamina as high as possible.

BE CAREFUL! Legends say a horrible creature guards the treasure. No one has ever made it back alive from the cavern to describe it, but on some, nearby villagers recount hearing terrifying growls from deep within.

You will start your journey with 3 stamina points. If at any point in your journey you lose all your stamina, you will die and the cavern will claim your soul...

Are you ready to begin what may well be your last adventure?
