The Entrance

You walk into the cavern's entrance. It is already getting dark, so you light up your torch. You walk a few steps and get to a T-shaped juncture in the path.

On the wall right in front of you, you notice some scribbles. Upon closer look, you can read:

"If the crystal you seek, best be prepared! Should you go Left, perils would you face! Should you go Right, night would be lit with day"

Right under the scribbles, you notice something written in finer print:

"Bluff you, would we dare? Or double-dare? Or triple-dare?"

Based on what you have just read, you must decide...

Go left Go right
