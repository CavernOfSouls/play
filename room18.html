BELZEGUUR

You enter the demon's catacomb, and soon realize that it makes up the majority of the pyramid's volume. The ceiling is dozens of meters heigh. The ground is composed of black rocks, on which are scattered a few boulders and bone piles. In front of you, the rocks drop down to form a ditch, beyond which you see a lake of lava.

Crouching on top of a boulder near the lava, you spot a large creature. It is almost five times as large as you. While it looks humanoid, it has the wings of a dragon, the fur of a wolf and the face of a bat.

You approach slowly - the Beast is currently feeding and has not yet noticed you. You reach for the crossbow strapped to your back, load it with an arrow, and fire at the Demon!

The arrow bounces of its skin and does no damage. There goes your advantage - Belzeguur swiftly turns around, its eyes burning like embers.

You fire two more arrows, but they are just as ineffective as the first. You must quickly find another weapon, or hide!

On the far size of the catacomb, you see a large axe, sitting atop a large rock. A few steps in front of you, you see a skeleton holding a rusty pocket knife. Near the entrance on your right, between Belzeguur and you, there is a crevace large enough for you to hide in, but too small for the Demon to reach you.

What do you go for? DECIDE NOW!
